02
What We Built
For the first time, users can generate a complete Wix site directly from their phone. No desktop required.
Before
Mobile visitors hit a wall. Account creation on mobile, site creation on desktop. The moment of intent gets split across two sessions, two devices, and often two days. Most people never come back.
After
From "I want a site" to "I have a site" — on the device where the decision was made. Enter a prompt. Wix AI generates the full site: structure, design, content. Ready to view, share, and publish.
Scope of this launch
  • Mobile site generation: full flow, from prompt to published site
  • View, share, and publish from mobile
  • Full editing remains on desktop (subsequent phase)
03
Who It's For
Primary segment
Mobile-first small business owners and creators who get the impulse to build a site while they're out living their business. Between appointments. At a market. On a commute. Not sitting at a desk.
Their situation
  • Goal: a professional online presence, fast
  • Pain: every tool requires a device they don't have in hand right now
  • Fix: the gap between "I need a site" and "I have a site" collapses to minutes
Out of scope (this launch)
Full site editing from mobile. The creation flow is the unlock. Editing is a subsequent phase.
These users already find us.

74% of homepage visits are mobile.
66% of Harmony visits are mobile and growing.

They have intent. We have been making them wait.
+31%
Mobile traffic growth M/M on Harmony. The audience is arriving faster. Conversion is not keeping up.

Competitive Landscape
Competitor Their Approach Their Weakness Our Edge
Squarespace Mobile app for editing only. Creation still desktop-first. No mobile creation flow. Same device-switch problem. We close the creation gap. They don't.
Webflow Desktop-native, developer-leaning. Mobile is an afterthought. Not built for mobile-first SMBs at all. Gap is industry-wide. We move first.
Shopify Strong mobile app for store management. Creation still desktop-heavy. Commerce-first. Not a site creation competitor directly. Creation moment is ours to own.
Framer / Tilda Design-forward, desktop-native. Power users only. No mobile creation. We serve the mass market they ignore.
We are the first major website builder to close the impulse-to-site gap on mobile. No competitor has solved mobile creation. They have mobile management. That is a different product for a different moment.

Risks & Mitigations
Risk Likelihood Impact Mitigation
Mobile-generated site quality is below the bar Medium High Hard gate: do not launch paid until quality is confirmed. Launch to organic first.
"Generate but not edit" frustrates users who want to customize immediately Medium Medium Set expectation in the flow: "Full editing on desktop." Frame as preview, not finished product.
Competitors ship mobile creation within 6 months Medium Medium Ship fast, capture the narrative first. Being first and known as first matters more.
Paid social creative underperforms Medium Medium Test 3+ creative angles in first 2 weeks before scaling. Demo, testimonial, problem-framing formats.
Lapsed user unsubscribe rate spikes Low Low Segment carefully (account created, no site built, last 12 months). One send, not a drip.
Biggest open question: do we have data on drop-off at the device-switch step? Quantify how many users created an account on mobile and never started a site on desktop. If that number exists, it belongs on Slide 1. Pull it before the leadership presentation.

# Data Brief: Mobile Funnel GTM Deck
**Prepared by:** Analyst Agent
**Date:** 2026-06-07
**Source:** Wix Marketing Growth Funnel KPIs dashboard, 28-day window ending 06 Jun 26
**Pages covered:** All pages (overall), Harmony page (wix.com/harmony), Homepage EN (wix.com/)

---

## The One-Sentence Story

Mobile drives the majority of traffic on every key Wix page, but converts at a fraction of desktop — and the gap is widest exactly where users are trying to create with AI.

---

## Data Summary by Page

### 1. Overall Funnel (All Pages)

| Device | Total Anons | Free/Anon Rate | Premium Rate | ACPU |
|--------|------------|----------------|--------------|------|
| Computer | 15.1M (+40% M/M) | 9.9% | 4.2% | $6.6 |
| Mobile | 11.5M (+10% M/M) | 5.5% | 5.6% | $4.7 |

- Mobile is 43% of all traffic.
- Mobile converts to free at roughly half the rate of desktop (5.5% vs 9.9%).
- Mobile premium rate (5.6%) is actually higher than desktop (4.2%). Users who do convert on mobile have stronger intent.
- Mobile premiums are up +26% M/M. The segment is growing and monetizing.

---

### 2. Harmony Page (wix.com/harmony — AI creation, prompt-box entry)

| Device | Total Anons | Free/Anon Rate | Premium Rate | Notes |
|--------|------------|----------------|--------------|-------|
| Mobile | 37.4K (+31% M/M) | 2.0% | 1.4% | Growing fast, barely converting |
| Computer | 18.8K (+9% M/M) | 17.9% | 1.4% | Converts at 9x the mobile rate |

- Mobile is 66% of Harmony traffic and growing faster (+31% vs +9%).
- Conversion gap: 2.0% mobile vs 17.9% desktop. A 9x difference.
- Context: Harmony's entry point is a text prompt box. On desktop, users type. On mobile, the friction kills the moment.
- This is the sharpest data point in the deck. The highest-intent AI creation page loses 2 out of 3 visitors because they're on mobile.

---

### 3. Homepage EN (wix.com/, English)

| Device | Total Anons | Free/Anon Rate | Premium Rate | ACPU |
|--------|------------|----------------|--------------|------|
| Mobile | 2.8M (+15% M/M) | 4.0% | 2.7% | $4.2 |
| Computer | 1.0M (+10% M/M) | 34.1% | 5.0% | $9.3 |

- Mobile is 74% of homepage traffic.
- Homepage conversion gap: 4.0% mobile vs 34.1% desktop. An 8.5x difference.
- Mobile homepage frees are still up +6% M/M despite the low conversion rate — volume is so high it still produces nearly 100K frees/month.
- Desktop ACPU ($9.3) is more than 2x mobile ACPU ($4.2) — but this reflects the current product limitation, not audience quality.

---

## Cross-Page Pattern

The pattern is consistent across all three data sets:

| Page | Mobile Traffic Share | Mobile Free/Anon | Desktop Free/Anon | Gap |
|------|---------------------|-----------------|-------------------|-----|
| All pages | 43% | 5.5% | 9.9% | 1.8x |
| Harmony | 66% | 2.0% | 17.9% | 9x |
| Homepage EN | 74% | 4.0% | 34.1% | 8.5x |

Mobile dominates visits. Desktop dominates conversions. The gap is not an audience problem — mobile users who get through convert at equal or higher premium rates than desktop. It is a product-to-device mismatch problem.

## Slides This Data Powers

**Slide 1 (Problem / Market Moment):**
- "74% of people who visit our homepage arrive on mobile. Only 4% of them sign up. Desktop converts at 34%."
- "On the Harmony page — our AI creation entry point — mobile traffic is 66% of all visits and growing at 31% month over month. Mobile conversion: 2%. Desktop: 17.9%."
- The cost of inaction: we are growing mobile traffic aggressively while leaving the conversion gap open.

**Slide 3 (Who It's For):**
- The mobile visitor who has intent (they came to Harmony, they know what AI creation means) but hits friction before they can act.
- Mobile users who do convert have a higher premium rate (5.6%) than desktop (4.2%). This is the audience quality signal.

**Slide 8 (Success Metrics):**
- Primary metric: Mobile Free/Anon rate on Harmony page. Baseline: 2.0%. Target: close toward 10%+ (meaningful movement toward desktop parity).
- Supporting: Mobile Free/Anon on Homepage EN (baseline 4.0%), Mobile Premiums M/M growth (currently +26%).

---

## Flags and Caveats

- All data is 28-day window. Trends are directional, not definitive.
- Harmony page sample is small (56.8K total anons vs. 4.4M for homepage). Conversion rates can swing more on small samples.
- "Premium rate higher on mobile" is a strong signal but should be validated: it may reflect a selection effect (only the most motivated mobile users push through the friction to convert).
- The Harmony prompt-box is a specific UX constraint. Mobile Funnel changes the creation flow, not just the device. Worth noting in the deck that the new flow is optimized for mobile input (not just a desktop flow on a smaller screen).

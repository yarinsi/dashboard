# Researcher Brief

## Angle 1: The 6-Month Proof
Half a year in, Wix Harmony users are building full sites in under an hour without touching a single line of code. AI drafts the layout, you drag it into yours.
Grounding: 6-month milestone + core Harmony promise: AI-generated starting point + full drag-and-drop override.

## Angle 2: Your Vibe, Not a Template
The frustration with Squarespace and old editors was the same: it looked like everyone else's site. Harmony's AI generates from your actual business description, not a category dropdown, so the output starts personal.
Grounding: Direct contrast with what the audience has already rejected (Squarespace, template pickers).

## Angle 3: Control Without a Developer
Small business owners who fired their freelancer still needed someone to call every time something changed. Harmony closes that gap: every AI-generated element stays fully editable. No code, no ticket, no waiting.
Grounding: The specific pain of post-freelancer dependency.

## Surprise Angle: The Undo Button Is the Feature
Everyone talks about what AI builds. Nobody talks about how easy it is to overrule it. The real unlock isn't the AI draft — it's that drag-and-drop means the AI never gets the final word. That's what control-anxious owners need to trust the tool.
Grounding: Reframes control as the hero, not AI — unexpected for a product launch recap post.

# Gatekeeper Review

Status: APPROVED

## What Works
- "Jab, jab, cross" cadence is tight throughout. "No code, no developer, no waiting on anyone" lands exactly right.
- Closing move is clean and confident. "Under an hour. That's Wix Harmony." No over-explanation, strong brand-stamp.
- Zero banned terms. Voice stays grounded in the small business owner's actual experience, not feature-speak.

## What to Watch
"Every element stays yours to move, resize, and rethink" runs slightly long — watch if the line starts drifting toward product-manual tone in future iterations.
